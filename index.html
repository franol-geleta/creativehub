Shaping a future that works for all

Impact Hub Berlin is the home for social entrepreneurship in the German capital. We are a catalyst for social innovation – a community, a consultancy and a creative space. As part of the biggest global network for social innovation, we inspire, connect and enable our local community of changemakers to develop solutions to the greatest challenges facing people and the planet

Welcome to Impact Hub Berlin

We provide impact-driven professionals with access to training and support, workspaces, workshops and networking events. With an international team, we also create professional incubation, acceleration, and innovation programmes for local and global organisations from diverse sectors, working at the intersection of society, economics and politics.

#WirVsVirus

Extraordinary times call for extraordinary innovation. Together with six other partners and supported by the German federal government, we created the #WirVsVirus 48-hour virtual hackathon to crowdsource solutions to mitigate the Covid-19 crisis. This led to several teams being supported to bring their ideas into reality through our follow-up Solution Builder support programme.
